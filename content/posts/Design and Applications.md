Building applications has come to a point of shipping tons of features. And while I do love more features, I have become painstakingly aware of friction. Friction of use, friction of visuals, friction of ease. Things have become convoluted - even overstimulating.

One could argue that we need a sort of resurgence of new ideas - but of less ideas, if that makes sense.
